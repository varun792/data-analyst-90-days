# What is Data Analytics?

## 1. What is data analytics?
a data analysts job is to define a problem , use the data and find a solution for the problem.

## 2. Types of analysts

### Data Analyst
- Input: clean, organise and arrange data  
- Output: raw data is changed into reports and dashboards. 
- Tools: SQL, python, tableau, excel & etc.

### Business Analyst
- Input: read and analyse the data 
- Output: find solution for the said problems using the data. 
- Tools: SQL, MS, communication skills & etc.

### BI Analyst
- Input: helps present the data in visual format.
- Output: visualised data is used to take actionable decision.
- Tools: SQL, power bi & etc.

## 3. What analysts do daily
- gather data.   
- clean the data.
- analyse the data.
- find patterns.
- create reports and dashboards for actionable decision. 

## 4. What analysts do NOT do
- built software's.
- coding 

## 5. Hiring reality (India-focused)
- its a competitive field.

- local companies in India are not as data driven as global MNC's.
